Adopting Satos is important
In Puerto Rico, Sato is the word used to describe a stray dog and over the island of Puerto Rico there are countless stray dogs that are in desperate need of a home. With your support these helpless animals can go on to live happy lives in loving homes. Just take Luna here as an example.
